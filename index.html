Kenny

Kenny, also known as Ken, is one of the main characters in Telltale Games' The Walking Dead. He is a surviving individual in the video game story's contextual apocalpyse alongside his family. He's an expressive person and is known for showing initiative and loyalty to those he cares for although his short temper and failure to think things through often get the better of him. He can be a good friend or distrustful ally to Lee Everett, protagonist, depending on the choices the player makes, but he is overall a family man that puts those close to him over himself.
